Location

This luxury hotel is located in the shopping, dining, and entertainment hub of Orchard Road, a 2 minute walk to ION Orchard, Orchard MRT Train Station, and a short drive to the central business district areas in City Hall, Raffles place, and Marina Bay.

What's special about Hotel Features?
